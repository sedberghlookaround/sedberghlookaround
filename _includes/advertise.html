Advertise & Contribute

The Sedbergh Lookaround is a volunteer led community newsletter. As such adverts are very attractively priced and we offer additional support including a write up for new businesses.

There is also the opportunity to contribute an article to the magazine should you be hosting an event or have some news relating to your service that is of general interest.

If you think you might want to commission an advert please read our advertisers information sheet. Should you choose to go ahead, please fill out and return our application form.

For articles, we recommend contacting us with your idea prior to submitting written content.
